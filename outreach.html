EXCEL Game Night #1

December 13, 2019

Join us at the parent teacher organization hosted EXCEL game night on 12/13, where we'll present our robot along with Union High School at Illahee Elementary School!

Visit Illahee Elementary School from 6 PM to 8 PM, where it'll be held in the school.

Girls in STEM

November 9, 2019

On 11/9, we will be collaborating with Union High School to encourage girls in STEM at Heritage High School under the Evergreen CTE department, and there will be a variety of experiments as well as an opportunity to try and drive our robot!

Visit Heritage High School from 10 AM to 3 PM, where events will be held across the school.

League Competition #1

November 2, 2019

On 11/2, we have our first FTC League competition. We'll be testing out our robot and seeing how it fares, so we can improve on later iterations.

Visit it at Covington Middle School from 9 AM to 3 PM, where it'll be held in the cafeteria/gym area.

Storming through Science

October 19, 2019

On 10/19, we'll be holding a miniature science fair at the library to show our robot and LEGO robots. Experiments involving electrostatics, properties of water, density, acidity/alkalinity, tension, and aerodynamics will be showcased as well.

Visit us at Cascade Park Library from 2 PM to 4 PM, in the large conference room (on the left of the entrance).
